Brand Story

HOW PETERSON BEGAN

Peterson started with a simple observation: quality shouldn't be a luxury. Founded by an entrepreneur with a passion for both sports and authentic natural products, Peterson set out to bridge the gap between premium quality and accessibility.

What began as a small operation in jerseys quickly expanded when customers started asking for reliable, natural palm oil. Two categories, one vision — bringing the best of both worlds directly to Nigerian homes and sport fans.

Today, Peterson serves thousands of satisfied customers across Nigeria, delivering premium jerseys from top clubs and leagues, and 100% natural palm oil processed with care and quality control. Every order is a promise kept.

RETURN & REFUND POLICY

We want you to be 100% satisfied with every purchase.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for jerseys in original, unworn condition with all tags attached. Palm oil products cannot be returned once delivered due to food safety regulations.

Refund Policy

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method. For bank transfers, refunds are made to the account provided during checkout. For Paystack payments, refunds are processed directly.

How to Request a Return

  1. Contact us on WhatsApp at 09033992088 within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your Order ID and photos showing the issue.
  3. We will confirm eligibility and arrange collection.
  4. Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed.
